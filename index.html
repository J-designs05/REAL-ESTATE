ABOUT US

Construst Limited is a CAC-registered liability company. Our standards are measured by the quality of the services we render. We are a young team of passionate and skilled professionals with immense experience in building, interior work, furniture, and external finishes such as stamped concrete floors, interlocking, and landscaping.

Our goal is to bring into reality the wishes of our clients and partners in record time to have a good experience through standard delivery, specifications, and good innovative ideas.

Our quality of work has captured the attention of the construction community and the elite society with a standard where durability and stability are assured.

Through our client partnership, intelligence, probity, innovation, and quality approach to projects, we have successfully executed and delivered top-of-the-line projects for reputable organizations and individuals.
